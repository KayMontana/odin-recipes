Kai's Lasagna



Description

Kai's lasagna will make your tastebuds jump around for joy. This euphoria inducing meal is something you will really love. The perfect combination of tomato sauce, meat, and cheeses make this recipe what it is. After eating this, you won't soon forget the taste.


Ingredients

Lasagna Noodles

Ground Beef

Tomato Sauce

Onions

Red Peppers

Yellow Peppers

Salt

Cottage Cheese

Mozarella Cheese


Steps


  1. First, you need to prepare your sauce. Put your ground beef in a pot and cook it up with a good amount of tomato sauce. Add onions for taste and add red and yellow peppers to make it more colorful and add some texture. Add some salt to the mix for extra taste, and any other seasonings you may like.



  2. Second, you need to build your lasagna. I like to do this in layers. One layer, from bottom to top goes like this: Meat Sauce, Noodle, Meat Sauce, Mozzarella Cheese, Cottage Cheese, Noodle. You want to build at least two layers of that, depending how tall of a lasagna you want. Be sure to add more mozarella on top to melt in and give the lasagna look.



  3. Third and finally, you want to bake your lasagna. You should bake it at 350 degrees for roughly 45 minutes. Then you let it cool and serve it up!!
