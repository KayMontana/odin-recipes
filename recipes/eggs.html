Scrambled eggs seem like the easiest thing to make, so here we are.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Milk

Steps

  1. First, you need to prepare your eggs. You should crack your desired amount of eggs into a bowl, so you can beat them. Beat them, and then add salt and pepper. I prefer my eggs pretty salty. Add milk to make your eggs a little more fluffy when they are finished.

  2. Second, you need to cook your eggs. Once again, I use a frying pan. No sidewalk or any fancy bullshit. I pour my eggs into the pan, and I use a spoon to scramble them. When it cooks a bit and starts to stick to the pan, that's when you use the spoon to scrape the egg and scramble it around and make it all nice.

  3. Finally, put your finished scrambled eggs in a plate. EAT.
