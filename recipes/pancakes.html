Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Baking Powder
  • Buttermilk
  • Sugar
  • Protein Powder
  • Salt
  • Oreos
  • Nutella
  • Milk
  • Butter

Steps

  1. First, you need to prepare your batter. 3 cups of sifted flour, one teaspoon of baking Soda and one teaspoon of baking powder. Mix that in one container. In another container, mix 3 teaspoons of butter with 3 tablespoons of sugar. Add a cup of buttermilk to this, and mix it together. Add your buttermilk concoction to your dry mixture, and mix until smooth and consitent, but make sure you don't mix too thin. Now, add one or two scoops of your preferred protein powder, depending on how much you want. Crush up a few Oreos and add them to the batter.

  2. Second, you need to cook your pancakes. I personally use a frying pan. Pour your batter into the frying pan, on a medium heat, and when the bottom starts to appear brown, flip it. You may see some bubbles, which will tell you that it is in fact cooking. After you flip it, let it cook all the way through and place all of your desired pancakes into a plate.

  3. Third and last, your nutella sauce. Mix nutella with milk until it has a saucy consistenty. You may microwave your sauce, but not for too long. Drench your pancakes in this sauce, and enjoy.
